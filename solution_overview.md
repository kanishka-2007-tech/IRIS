## Solution Overview

The AI-Based Smart Safety & Crime Prevention System provides a proactive safety framework
using artificial intelligence and data analytics.

### Key Components:
- AI-powered crime risk prediction
- Real-time dynamic risk mapping
- Safe route recommendations
- Proactive risk alerts
- Voice-activated emergency assistance
- The SOS click and the alert is sent to the registered mobile number with location

The system processes crime-related data and generates risk levels for different locations
and time windows, enabling users to make informed safety decisions.
